A Smarter Way to Handle Security Alerts

A proposed framework for an efficient, secure, and auditable SOC workflow.

The Opportunity: From Good to Great

We have powerful tools like ReliaQuest and Defender. By formalizing our alert handling process, we can unlock new levels of efficiency and security.

The Challenge: A One-Size-Fits-All Process

Currently, our informal process treats every alert similarly. This can cause delays for critical threats while creating unnecessary work for minor, contained incidents. Analysts often wear two hats: investigator and remediator.

Our Goal: To mature our workflow, reduce risk, and create a robust audit trail using the tools and team we already have.
